Our Events

Engagement 9.02.2021
Deemed one of the most important pre wedding ceremonies not only in Hindu weddings but in other religions as well, the event of engagement is mostly an intimate one with close relatives from both bride and groom's side.The bride and the groom then exchange rings during to solidify their bond
Lagan 12.02.2021
Tilak Ceremony is reckoned as the first step to the bond between the two families. This auspicious event takes place mostly in the groom's residence, where the male members of the bride's family visit to put Kumkum or vermilion on his forehead.
Haldi 15.02.2021
Amongst the most fun events during an Indian wedding is the Haldi Ceremony.In this ceremony a paste of haldi is applied on the bride and the groom's body which accentuate the bride and the groom for their big day. The colour yellow is also considered auspicious, and thus is said to ward off the evil.
Mehandi 15.02.2021
Mehendi Ceremony is about applying Henna to the bride's hand and feet. This beautiful event takes place usually in the evening amidst a lot of dancing and music by the family members and friends.
Phere 16.02.2021
The seven vows can be said to contain the crux of the practice of marriage. These seven vows are the seven promises which the bride and the groom make to each other for a happy and prosperous life.They are bound together by an unbreakable bond.
